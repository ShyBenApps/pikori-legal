Account and Data Deletion for Pikori

You can delete your Pikori account and associated data directly inside the app. You can also delete or request deletion of specific data without deleting your entire account.

How to delete your account in the app

  1. Open Pikori.
  2. Go to your profile.
  3. Open the settings menu.
  4. Select “Delete account”.
  5. Confirm the deletion request.

If you cannot access the app

If you can no longer access your account or the app, you can request account deletion by contacting us at pikori.help@outlook.com.

Please contact us from the email address connected to your Pikori account, so we can verify the request.

Data deleted when your account is deleted

When your account is deleted, we delete or anonymize account-related data associated with your Pikori account, including:

  • your profile information, including age or eligibility information where stored
  • your avatar
  • your uploaded submissions and related media
  • bookmarks, friendships, votes, achievements, reports, and related app data

How to delete specific data without deleting your account

You can remove some data without deleting your full Pikori account.

  • You can delete your own submissions in the app from the submission detail page.
  • You can change or remove your profile picture/avatar in the profile settings.
  • You can remove bookmarks in the app.
  • You can remove friends in the app.
  • You can manage blocked users in the app.

If you want us to delete specific data that you cannot remove yourself in the app, contact us at pikori.help@outlook.com.

Please describe exactly which data you want deleted and contact us from the email address connected to your Pikori account.

Data that may be retained

Some limited information may be retained where necessary for security, fraud prevention, abuse prevention, child safety, legal obligations, dispute handling, or enforcement of our rules.

Backup copies and technical logs may remain for a limited period before being deleted. We aim to process deletion requests within 30 days, unless a longer period is required by law or for legitimate safety or security reasons.

Contact

If you have questions about account or data deletion, contact us at pikori.help@outlook.com.
