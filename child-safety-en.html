Child Safety Standards for Pikori

Pikori has zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation, including child sexual abuse material.

Users may not upload, share, request, promote, or distribute any content that:

  • sexually exploits, abuses, or endangers children
  • sexualizes minors in any way
  • depicts, promotes, requests, or distributes child sexual abuse material
  • encourages grooming, predatory behavior, or inappropriate contact with minors
  • attempts to evade moderation, reporting, or safety systems related to child safety

Reporting child safety concerns

Users can report submissions directly inside the Pikori app using the available report functions.

Additional safety concerns can also be reported by contacting us at pikori.help@outlook.com.

Moderation and enforcement

We review reports and may remove content, restrict features, suspend accounts, or permanently ban accounts where we reasonably believe these standards, our community rules, or applicable law have been violated.

Where required by law or where appropriate for user safety, we may preserve and report relevant information to regional, national, or other competent authorities.

Our commitment

Pikori is designed to be a creative and safe community. We are committed to preventing the availability of child sexual abuse and exploitation content on our service and to taking appropriate action when such content or behavior is reported or detected.
