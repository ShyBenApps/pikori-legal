Privacy Policy for Pikori

Last updated: [DATE]

This Privacy Policy explains how ShyBen Apps (“we”, “us”, “our”) processes personal data when you use Pikori (the “App”).

1. Controller

The controller responsible for processing your personal data is:

ShyBen Apps
Austria
Email: shybenapps@outlook.com

2. What data we process

Depending on how you use the App, we may process the following categories of personal data:

  • Account data, such as your email address and authentication-related identifiers
  • Profile data, such as your username, handle, avatar, profile settings, and discovery preferences
  • User-generated content, such as uploaded images, videos, thumbnails, and related metadata
  • Participation data, such as submissions, votes, bookmarks, achievements, leaderboard placements, streaks, friendships, and challenge participation
  • Moderation and safety data, such as reports, account restrictions, bans, moderation actions, and related administrative records
  • Feedback and support data, such as bug reports, feature requests, app version, and platform information
  • Technical and security data, such as logs, service events, and information needed to maintain app security, reliability, and abuse prevention
  • Advertising-related data, including data processed in connection with ads displayed in the App

3. Why we process your data

We process personal data in order to:

  • create and manage user accounts
  • provide core app features such as profiles, submissions, voting, leaderboards, achievements, bookmarks, friendships, and challenge participation
  • store, display, and process user-generated content within the App
  • review reports, moderate content, enforce our rules, and protect users
  • prevent fraud, abuse, spam, vote manipulation, and security incidents
  • respond to support requests and user feedback
  • maintain, troubleshoot, and improve the functionality, safety, and reliability of the App
  • display and manage advertising in the App
  • comply with legal obligations and defend legal claims

4. Legal bases

Where required by applicable law, we rely on one or more of the following legal bases:

  • Performance of a contract: where processing is necessary to provide the App and its core functionality
  • Legitimate interests: where processing is necessary for security, moderation, abuse prevention, service integrity, and related operational purposes
  • Legal obligation: where processing is necessary to comply with applicable law
  • Consent: where required for optional processing, including certain advertising or privacy choices

Where we rely on consent, you may withdraw that consent for future processing at any time.

5. Advertising

The App may display ads, including ads provided by third-party advertising partners such as Google AdMob.

These providers may process device, app, and advertising-related information in order to serve, measure, and, where permitted, personalize ads.

Where required by law, we will request consent before using personal data for personalized advertising or related purposes.

6. Camera access

If you choose to use features that capture photos or videos, the App may request access to your device camera.

Camera access is used only to enable features you actively choose to use, such as taking a profile photo or capturing content for a submission.

7. Who receives data

We may share personal data with:

  • hosting, database, authentication, and storage providers
  • infrastructure, support, and security providers
  • advertising and related measurement providers
  • analytics or crash-reporting providers, if used
  • authorities, advisers, or other third parties where required by law or reasonably necessary to protect rights, safety, or the integrity of the service
  • other users of the App, where your profile, avatar, submissions, rankings, or related activity are visible inside the App

8. International transfers

Where personal data is transferred outside the EEA, we will use appropriate safeguards where required by law, such as adequacy decisions or approved contractual safeguards.

9. How long we keep data

We keep personal data only for as long as necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy.

In general:

  • account and profile data are kept while your account remains active
  • uploaded content is kept while your account remains active, unless removed earlier by you, by moderation, or by operational necessity
  • reports may be retained in de-linked or partially de-linked form where necessary for moderation, abuse prevention, recordkeeping, or legal defense
  • technical logs, security records, and backup copies may remain temporarily after deletion and are removed or allowed to expire according to infrastructure retention practices

10. Account deletion

You can request deletion of your account from within the App.

When your account deletion is processed, we delete your active account data from the App’s live systems, including your profile and associated uploaded media, and we also delete the related authentication account.

Some information may remain temporarily or in limited form, including:

  • de-linked report records
  • temporary backup copies
  • limited logs or records needed for legal compliance, security, fraud prevention, moderation, or the establishment, exercise, or defense of legal claims

We process deletion promptly in normal operation, but backup and infrastructure copies may expire later rather than being removed instantly in every system.

11. Service shutdown

If we permanently discontinue the App, we may delete accounts, uploaded content, and related personal data from active systems as part of the shutdown process.

Where practicable, we may provide advance notice.

Limited data may remain temporarily in backups, logs, or records required for legal, security, fraud-prevention, or compliance purposes.

12. Your rights

Subject to applicable law, you may have the right to:

  • access your personal data
  • correct inaccurate personal data
  • request deletion of your personal data
  • restrict certain processing
  • object to certain processing
  • receive a portable copy of certain data
  • withdraw consent where consent is the basis of processing
  • lodge a complaint with a competent supervisory authority

13. Children

Pikori is not intended for users under the age of 14.

14. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures designed to protect personal data against unauthorized access, misuse, loss, or alteration. However, no method of storage or transmission is completely secure.

15. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If changes are material, we will provide reasonable notice in the App or by other appropriate means.
