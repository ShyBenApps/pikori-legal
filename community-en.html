Community Rules for Pikori

We want Pikori to be creative, competitive, and safe.

You may not upload, share, or promote content that:

  • is illegal
  • is sexually explicit, exploitative, or otherwise prohibited by law or our safety standards
  • depicts or promotes abuse, self-harm, or extreme violence
  • contains hate speech, harassment, threats, bullying, or intimidation
  • violates another person’s privacy
  • infringes another person’s copyright, trademark, or other rights
  • impersonates another person or misleads others about identity
  • is spam, scam, manipulative, or intended to game rankings, votes, reports, or platform systems
  • sexualizes or exploits minors in any way

You may only upload content that you created yourself or that you have permission to use.

You must also behave respectfully when participating in the community. Attempts to abuse reporting, voting, moderation systems, or other users may lead to restrictions or bans.

We may remove content, reduce visibility, restrict features, suspend accounts, or permanently ban accounts where we reasonably believe these rules or applicable law have been violated.

If you believe content or an account violates these rules, please use the in-app report function or contact us at shybenapps@outlook.com.
