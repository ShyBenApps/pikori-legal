Community Rules for Pikori

We want Pikori to be creative, competitive, and safe for users aged 13 and older.

All challenges, submissions, avatars, usernames, handles, profiles, and other content on Pikori must be suitable for users aged 13 and older, including younger teens.

You may not upload, share, submit, or promote content that:

  • is illegal
  • contains nudity, sexual content, sexually explicit content, or sexually suggestive content
  • sexualizes, exploits, endangers, or targets minors in any way
  • depicts or promotes abuse, self-harm, suicide, gore, extreme violence, or dangerous acts
  • promotes alcohol, tobacco, vaping, drugs, weapons, or illegal activity
  • contains hate speech, harassment, threats, bullying, intimidation, humiliation, or targeted abuse
  • violates another person’s privacy, including addresses, phone numbers, school information, IDs, documents, license plates, or other personal information
  • shows another person without their permission
  • infringes another person’s copyright, trademark, privacy, publicity, or other rights
  • impersonates another person or misleads others about identity
  • is spam, scam, manipulative, or intended to game rankings, votes, reports, achievements, leaderboards, streaks, or platform systems
  • is otherwise unsafe, inappropriate, or unsuitable for users aged 13 and older

You may only upload content that you created yourself or that you have permission to use.

You must behave respectfully when participating in the community. Attempts to abuse reporting, voting, moderation systems, downloads, friend requests, blocking, or other users may lead to restrictions or bans.

If you see a submission, profile, or behavior that violates these rules, please use the in-app report function or contact us at pikori.help@outlook.com.

You can also block other users where this feature is available.

We may remove content, reduce visibility, restrict features, suspend or permanently ban accounts, or delete accounts and related media where we reasonably believe these rules, our Terms, our Child Safety Standards, or applicable law have been violated.
